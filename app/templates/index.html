jaxsen

jaxsen is an eccentric artist who creates alternative electronic music in an attempt to understand himself and the world around him. Growing up, his life was centered around two things: his love for computers and the invisible war in his mind. These pillars were built upon and have grown into a plethora of synth-based, reflective, melancholiac songs. Influenced by lyricists like Lorde and girl in red and producers like Grimes and dandelion hands, jaxsen has developed a unique sound that has been described as reminiscent of Modest Mouse and Radiohead. Each song provides insight into the thoughts and feelings of a lad just trying to figure things out.
